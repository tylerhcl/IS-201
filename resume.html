My Repertoire
2023 - Present
BYU Symphony Orchestra
Principal Cellist
Provo, UT
As the principal cellist in the auditioned BYU Symphony Orchestra, I’ve had the opportunity to lead a talented section of musicians through a wide range of classical repertoire. This role has challenged me musically and personally, demanding a high level of preparation, leadership, and communication. Beyond just playing, I take responsibility for bowings, style, and section unity, ensuring that we contribute to the overall sound and success of the ensemble. Working closely with the conductor and fellow section leaders has deepened my appreciation for orchestral collaboration at a collegiate level.
2017 - 2021
TMHS Chamber Orchestra
Principal Cellist
Tomball, TX
During my time at TMHS, I served as the principal cellist in the Chamber Orchestra, an advanced ensemble made up of the school’s top musicians. In this role, I helped guide the cello section through regional and state-level competitions, as well as numerous performances. It was here that I learned the importance of setting a musical example and supporting my peers through positive leadership. These formative years played a critical role in shaping my musical discipline and laying the groundwork for future performance opportunities.

Accomplishments

2025
Soloist with BYU Symphony Orchestra
Provo, UT
One of my most memorable experiences as a cellist has been performing two featured solos in concert with the BYU Symphony Orchestra. Preparing and delivering a solo performance with a full ensemble behind me was both thrilling and humbling. These performances not only tested my technical and expressive abilities, but also gave me a chance to share my voice and musical interpretation with a large audience. The experience solidified my confidence and deepened my passion for performance.
2018
All-State Region Orchestra
Houston, TX
After a rigorous audition process, I earned a spot in the prestigious TMEA All-State Region Orchestra. This experience brought together some of the most dedicated young musicians in Texas, offering a weekend of intense rehearsals and a final performance under a distinguished conductor. Being a part of this ensemble was both motivating and inspiring—it pushed me to grow in skill, precision, and professionalism while connecting with other passionate student musicians from across the region.
